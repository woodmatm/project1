Our Mission

We want to help YOU improve at the games YOU love

WeCoach is dedicated to helping players get better at video games and esports they love most

We want to help improve gaming experiences by unlocking potential

Every player has a unique set of objectives they try to accomplish when they play games. Some of these players have plans to go pro, while other might just simply enjoy the journey of progress within their game. We wanted to create the best learning platform for our students by maximising the gaming experience. We have coaches that have been 5+ years experience coaching in each of our supported games.
